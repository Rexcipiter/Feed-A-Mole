Recipe for Success:

  1. Have brilliant idea (probably while driving or in shower).
  2. Have torrent of world-shattering ideas compound with original idea.
  3. Panic because you realize have no way to actually record any of these ideas at the moment.
  4. Make desperate attempt to voice command a note to your phone knowing you're about to lose track of the entire train of thought.
  5. Be thankful you salvaged something out of the treasure trove of thoughts and fade back into annoyance at your lack of world-wide fame and fortune.
